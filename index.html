Welcome to Kaleido

The Ultimate Volunteering Site

Our goal is to make volunteering easy and accessible to those who hope to make their community better. Whether it be through providing transportation support to marginalized communities that feel unsafe traveling or helping stray animals find a new home, we have something for everyone willing to help.

General Opportunities

These volunteering opportunities are not affiliated with Kaleido, but are a perfect way to expand the ways you give back to your community. With a mix of remote and physical opportunities to choose from, you'll always find something that fits your availability and interest.

Stranimals

Volunteer to work at an animal shelter, act as a foster parent to an animal of your choice, or simply help raise awareness for strays in your community. When you search for strays, you'll be met with the top 20 animals that need immediate help. Click one and contact the current caretaker using the number provided for further instruction.

OK-Transport

OK-Transport is created by and made for marginalized groups that may feel unsafe when traveling. Our drivers are thoroughly background checked and we ensure that you will have a safe, reliable, and comfortable ride. Simply look for an available driver, place your request, and wait for your driver.

H&C Mentoring

Have a small question that you're embarrassed to ask? Don't worry. We've been there. Whether it be about laundry or needing tutoring for an upcoming test, our H&C Fellows have you covered. Fill out a three question questionnaire and get matched with an available mentor or tutor!
